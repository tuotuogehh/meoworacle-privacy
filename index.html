Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Meow Pause is designed to work offline and to respect your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

We do not require an account and do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. The app does not ask you to enter personal thoughts or journal text. We do not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Data Stored on Your Device

Saved reflections, review history and timestamps, preferences, and first-launch status are stored locally in the app sandbox on your device. You can remove all app data by deleting the app. This information is not sent to us.

In-App Purchases

Optional purchases and restorations are processed by Apple through StoreKit and may require an internet connection. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Photos and Sharing

The app can create an image of a reflection card. Saving to Photos or sharing happens only after you choose the corresponding system action. Apple’s system share sheet controls the destination. We do not receive the shared content.

External Links

Optional links may open your email app or the App Store. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

Children

The app is suitable for a general audience and knowingly collects no personal information from children.

Entertainment Disclaimer

Meow Pause is intended only for entertainment and self-reflection. Its reflection cards are not medical, legal, financial, or other professional advice and should not be used as a substitute for qualified professional guidance.

GDPR and CCPA

Because we do not collect or process personal information, there is no personal information held by us to access, correct, delete, sell, or opt out from. If this changes, this policy and the app’s consent mechanisms will be updated before collection begins.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be revised.
